I enjoy being the Guardian to these four elements known as my clowder. They keep me grounded and they are quite the comedians. I am currently training them to target a button for button training. Three out of four will work for treats.

My superpower is Patience. These little bundles of contradiction have been helping me perfect it.

Some of my other hobbies include reading, learning new things, knitting, drawing, painting and calligraphy.

I also enjoy yoga, swimming, tai chi, and taekwondo.

I like to travel but am a hermit and prefer the ocean or mountains over cities.
